Education and Programs

Holy Family School, HFS, is a private school that offers education for both the primary and secondary stages of scholastic academia. It is situated in Palestine, Gaza where it offers high level instructing of the Palestinian school curriculum. HFS has a very high standard for admission, and that is certainly for a reason. It focuses on the deep understanding of scientific subjects like Mathematics and Physics, while also demanding its students to exhibit a strong sense of creativity in subjects like Arabic and English literature. HFS puts an immense effort in embedding a rich experience into its students, molding their characters to be more strong and resilient. This is exactly how I, Zuhair, would describe being a student at Holy Family School.

Abraham Lincoln Program, administered by AMIDEAST, is a highly-competitive U.S government-funded program dedicated to enriching Palestinian school students in English, and engage with the community through many different ways. During the program, students undergo an extensive submerge in the English language through debates and discussions about different topics. It also puts students in different environments through civic engagement and activities in order to enhance their perceptive and social skills.

Bridge Palestine is a local program administered by “Natuf” organization whose cause is to educate young students in Palestine. It collects students from all over the country and puts them together in an attempt to push them out of their comfort zones and show their true colors. This program’s approach considers the very essence of learning, curiosity and discovery, and brings it to real life in a way tangible for the young students to deal with. Topics like climate change, co-education, and human rights are very present throughout the experience, which goes hand in hand with giving the students a pure, rich experience.

The Hope Fund. It is a program that focuses on getting a U.S higher education through admission into a U.S college. In this very competitive program, students get prepared for international testing like the SAT and TOEFL tests required for admission into a U.S higher education institution. It helps students with potential find an education abroad where they can indulge in their passions and have an opportunity to learn the field they choose.
